Features & Functionality

Five systems, one district-wide picture

From the moment a patient describes a symptom to the moment a district officer dispatches supplies, everything routes through one connected platform.

01

AI-Powered Patient Triage

Gemini AI reads typed or voice-transcribed symptoms and classifies each case RED, AMBER, or GREEN — with an immediate relocation directive like an ICU bed number for critical cases.

02

Live Resource Monitoring

A real-time grid and map view of beds, doctors, and medicine across every PHC and CHC, with 24-hour trend charts built on Recharts.

03

District Management Board

Automatic critical alerts when beds hit zero or stock falls below threshold, plus manually reported issues, all on one dashboard for district officers.

04

Authentication & Roles

Firebase Authentication with role-based views — Centre Admins and District Officers each see only what their role needs.

05

Data Persistence

Every triage record and management report is stored in Firebase Firestore, enabling historical auditing and longitudinal analysis.

06

Predictive Analytics

Beyond live snapshots, current consumption rates feed a 48-hour shortage forecast — so a clinic can request supplies before a bed or medicine actually runs out, not after.

End-to-End Workflow

How a case moves through the system

Four roles, one continuous path from a patient's first symptom to a district-level decision.

Any user

Authentication

The user signs in and their role determines what they can see — frontline worker, centre admin, or district officer.

Frontline workers

Triage

A patient arrives, symptoms are entered by text or voice, and the AI instantly returns a triage tier, action plan, and relocation directive. The record saves to the cloud automatically.

Centre admins

Resource Tracking

Admins check the Live Resource Grid for their own or nearby clinics, then switch to the Trends tab to request supplies ahead of the 48-hour forecast.

District officers

District Management

Officers watch the District Management Board for real-time alerts on full beds or critical stock, review manual reports, and dispatch ambulances or supplies.
